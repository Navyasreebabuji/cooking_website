chicken-biriyani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish that combines tender chicken marinated in spices and yogurt with fragrant basmati rice. Originating from South Asia, this one-pot meal is known for its rich layers of spices, saffron-infused rice, and the delightful fragrance of herbs like coriander and mint. Perfect for special occasions or a comforting family dinner, Chicken Biryani is beloved for its delicious blend of flavors and textures.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : meat food Lunch

instructions

step 1

Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, salt, and lemon juice. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes. Rinse and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Boil water with spices and salt, add soaked rice, and cook until 70% done. Drain and set aside.

step 2

Heat oil or ghee in a pot, fry sliced onions until golden, then add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft. Add the marinated chicken and cook until fully cooked. In the pot with chicken, layer half the rice, drizzle with saffron milk (optional), add the remaining rice, and garnish with coriander and mint.

step 3

Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat for 20 minutes to allow flavors to meld.

step 4

Fluff the rice gently and serve hot with raita or salad.

ingredients

500g chicken, cut into pieces 1 cup yogurt 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste 1 teaspoon red chili powder 1 teaspoon turmeric powder 1 teaspoon garam masala Salt and lemon juice

2 cups basmati rice 4 cups water Spices (bay leaves, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon) Salt

2 tablespoons oil or ghee 2 large onions, sliced 2 tomatoes, chopped Fresh coriander and mint leaves

tools

Large Heavy Pot With Lid
