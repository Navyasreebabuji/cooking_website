Banana Pancakes

Banana Pancakes are a delightful and easy breakfast option that combines the natural sweetness of ripe bananas with fluffy pancake batter. Perfect for a quick morning meal or a weekend brunch, these pancakes are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. They can be customized with various toppings like maple syrup, honey, fresh fruit, or nuts. With their soft texture and rich flavor, Banana Pancakes are sure to please everyone at the table!

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it.

step 2

Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown.

step 3

Enjoy warm with syrup, fruit, or your favorite toppings!

ingredients

1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix

1/2 cup flax seed meal

1 cup skim milk

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
