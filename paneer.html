panner-butter-masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich and creamy Indian dish made with soft paneer (Indian cottage cheese) simmered in a luscious tomato-based gravy. This beloved vegetarian classic is known for its delightful blend of spices, including garam masala, cumin, and coriander, which infuse the sauce with deep flavor. Finished with butter and cream, Paneer Butter Masala is a favorite in North Indian cuisine, often enjoyed with naan, roti, or rice. Its smooth, velvety texture and vibrant taste make it a perfect choice for special occasions or a comforting meal at home.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : breakfast Lunch food

instructions

step 1

In a pan, heat oil and 1 tablespoon of butter. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until fragrant.

step 2

Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture. Mix in red chili powder, garam masala, cumin powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.

step 3

Gently add paneer cubes and stir to coat them in the sauce. Pour in the cream and remaining butter. Mix well and simmer for a few minutes.

step 4

Garnish with fresh coriander before serving. Enjoy hot with naan, roti, or rice!

ingredients

250g paneer, cut into cubes 2 tablespoons butter 1 tablespoon oil 1 large onion, finely chopped 2-3 tomatoes, pureed

1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste 1 teaspoon red chili powder 1 teaspoon garam masala 1 teaspoon cumin powder

1/2 cup cream Salt to taste Fresh coriander for garnish

tools

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
